Methodology

From Idea to Live

A concise view of how strategies are engineered, validated, and risk‑controlled.

1

Idea Generation

Signal research from historical time series, order flow, and market microstructure.

2

Validation

Statistical significance, robustness checks, and regime sensitivity prior to any optimization.

3

Optimization

Parameter sweeps, walk‑forward analysis, and slippage modeling to avoid overfitting.

4

Backtesting

Multi‑year simulations with realistic costs: spreads, commissions, and partial fills.

5

Forward Testing

Paper/live‑shadow with DMA routing; monitor latency, queue position, and realized slippage.

6

Deployment

Colocation/DMA venues, health checks, and rollback triggers.

7

Risk Management

Drawdown controls, volatility targeting, circuit‑breakers, and fail‑safe systems.
